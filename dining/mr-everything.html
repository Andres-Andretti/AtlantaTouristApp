Mr. Everything

Attraction Details

Mr Everything Deli has been serving the AUC community and surrounding business appetites since 1993. The menu consists of a variety of sandwiches, salads, burgers, hotdogs, and rice dishes. All dishes come served grilled with onions, mushrooms, green peppers, broccoli, spinach, three cheeses, and tomatoes.
